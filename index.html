Cultivating a healthy you from the ground up.

I believe massage should be accessible to all people, not just those with a comfortable salary. I want to give back to my community through discounts &/or sliding scale payment options, especially for seniors and “essential” workers. I want to help improve the quality of daily life through pain reduction and increased freedom of movement. I want to work with anyone ready to take control of the work that needs to be done to accomplish these goals. - Anita Blount, LMT, PharmD
